About Rachael

most of the world is just beautiful nonsense anyway

Rachael Wright is a web developer currently enrolled in UCLA's Fullstack Web Development Bootcamp. Passionate about presentation and functionality of websites for both users and clients, she's enthusiastic about the ongoing learning involved with a field experiencing constant change.

Interested in website development since her pre-teen years, Rachael started out coding table layouts on Geocities before graduating to customising pages on Social Media and journaling sites, as well as designing simple generators for collaborative online community projects. She's since written interactive JavaScript-based monster fighting games and Express and Handlebars generated text-based games where user choices affect the outcomes.

Outside of coding, she loves photography, hiking, working with animals, and traveling to visit friends around the world.
